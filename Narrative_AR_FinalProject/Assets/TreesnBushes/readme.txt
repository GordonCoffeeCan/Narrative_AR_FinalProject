README
=======

LowPoly Trees and Bushes V2.0

This Asset contains 9 models with less than 1000 tris each:
3 trees, 3 medium size bushes and 3 small bushes
All models come with an ambient occlusion texture and shadow.
You'll find these in the 'Static/prefabs' folder.

NEW in Version 2.0
---------------------
We've added a morph to one of the trees. It grows in size and age in multiple ways now...
As it makes no sense to give it an ambient occlusion texture and shadow, it comes with 2 adjustable colors.
You'll find these in the 'Morph/prefabs' folder.
